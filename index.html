Léonard Hussenot's Homepage

I am a senior research scientist in the Google DeepMind team in Paris. Before that, I was very lucky to be a CIFRE PhD studen between Google Brain and INRIA Lille.

I am interested in leveraging human feedback for sequential decision making. This led me to explore ideas related to apprenticeship learning, imitation learning, and RL from human feedback. I am very proud to be a contributor of the Acme library. I also was heavily involved in the conception of Google's Bard .
